Meeting People

You don't get a phone book. You discover people through your Familiars:

1. A Familiar meets someone while exploring town

2. They tell you about the person and offer to introduce you

3. You request an introduction through the app

4. The person accepts (or doesn't) based on their personality

5. Once connected, you can message them directly

People respond at their own pace â€” once or twice a day for most, faster for urgent topics. Just like real life, some people are chatty and some take their time.

The more you talk to someone, the more you learn about them. Their profile fills in over time â€” likes, dislikes, history, and eventually, secrets.

Thornhaven is a small Pacific Northwest town with about 4,200 residents and 227+ named characters. It has real geography â€” distances between locations, daily schedules for every character, and travel times that matter.

Your Familiars move through this world at their own pace:
