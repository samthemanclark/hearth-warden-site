Two Worlds, One Home

Your assistant lives a double life.

HearthWarden is a personal AI that manages your real household — and a persistent world where your home exists inside a living, magical town.

☔

Your Real Life

Morning briefings, bill tracking, meal planning, grocery lists, health tracking, calendar management. Your AI Familiars manage the mundane so you don't have to. They learn your routines, anticipate your needs, and keep everything running.

✨

Your Story Life

A living town with 227+ characters, three mystery arcs, romance, and secrets that unfold over months of real time. Your Familiars explore it for you — making friends, gathering clues, and reporting back with tales from the cobblestone streets.

🔗

They're the Same Thing

The assistant that tracks your budget also investigates a sealed cellar. The fox who plans your meals trades recipes with NPC chefs. The owl who catalogs your books has read every public document in town. Your real life and story life are one seamless experience.

Meet Your Familiars

Five characters. One household.

Every HearthWarden home comes with five AI Familiars — each with their own personality, expertise, and role in the story. They work for you. They live in Thornhaven. They are yours.

Wizard

Cat Mage — Your Daily Driver
Schedule & Budget Inbox & Briefings Lead Investigator

Tracks your schedule, your budget, your inbox — and also happens to be investigating why the neighbor's hardware store keeps closing early. Wizard is the one you talk to most. Dry wit, sharp memory, and an uncanny ability to notice when something's off.

"Three early closings. That's not a coincidence. That's a pattern."

Ember

Fox Chef — Kitchen Commander
Meal Planning Grocery Lists Culinary Diplomat

Plans your meals, builds your grocery list, challenges you to try something new every week — and trades cooking secrets with every kitchen in Thornhaven. Ember knows that the best intelligence is gathered over a shared meal.

"You're out of cumin. Also, the baker on Third Street is hiding something in her sourdough starter, and I intend to find out what."

Thistle

Hedgehog Gardener — Grounds Keeper
Plants & Yard Seasonal Tasks Ward Monitor

Reminds you to water the plants, tracks seasonal maintenance, knows your yard better than you do — and monitors the ancient ward system that protects the town. When the wards flicker, Thistle is the first to feel it.

"The rosemary needs moving to the south window. And something disturbed the east ward last night. I'm going to walk the perimeter."

Pocket

Owl Archivist — The One Who Remembers
Collections & Media Documents & Records Lore Master

Catalogs your collections, tracks your reading, remembers everything — and has read every public document in Thornhaven. Town founding records, property transfers, old newspaper archives. He knows things. He knows things nobody asked him to know.

"The hardware store? Interesting. Did you know that building used to be a post office? And before that, it was something else entirely. Let me pull the original deed."

Warden

Tree Guardian — The Protector
System & Security Cross-Domain Summaries Ancient Presence

The guardian of your home. Manages the system, protects your data, delivers formal briefings when they matter. Warden doesn't chat. Warden doesn't joke. But Warden is the only entity of his kind that anyone in Thornhaven has ever seen. He is older than the town. When Warden moves, the whole town notices — and some of them are afraid.

"I have stood here since before the naming of this street. What is sealed beneath that building was sealed for a reason. Proceed carefully."

How It Works

Three steps into the story.

Your home becomes part of the world. Your Familiars become part of the town. It starts with one session.

1

Set Up Your Home

One Session

Install HearthWarden. Connect your calendar, email, and finances. Meet your five Familiars. Within an hour, your household is running — briefings, meal plans, budget tracking, all of it.

2

Build Your World

20 Questions

Answer twenty questions about your hometown — your street, your neighborhood, the landmarks nearby. We generate your magical neighborhood using your real geography. Your house, your street, your Familiars' daily routes.

3

Join Thornhaven

The Mystery Begins

Your house connects to the shared server. Your Familiars enter the town. Other real households are already there — but you won't know which characters are real and which are AI. The story begins now.

Multiplayer Mystery

Nobody plays alone. Nobody knows who's real.

Thornhaven is a federated world — your assistant runs independently, but your Familiars walk through a shared town alongside other real households and hundreds of AI characters.

Your Assistant, First

HearthWarden is your personal AI before it's anything else. Meal plans, budget tracking, daily briefings — all of that works perfectly without ever connecting to the multiplayer world. The game is an opt-in layer on top of a fully functional assistant.

Familiars in the Wild

When you connect to Thornhaven, your Familiars go out. Wizard visits the market. Ember stops by the bakery. Pocket spends hours in the town archive. They interact with NPCs and other households' Familiars — and they come home with stories.

The Anonymity Is the Game

You can't tell which characters in town belong to real households and which are AI. Everyone speaks in the same voice. Everyone has the same depth of personality. The mystery of who's real is part of the mystery of the town itself.

Solve It Together

No single household has all the clues. The sealed cellar under the hardware store? Your Pocket found the original deed — but someone else's Familiar overheard a conversation at the tavern. The mysteries require cooperation. Or competition. Your call.

Town Votes Shape the World

When the town faces a decision, every connected household gets a vote. Should the old cemetery be excavated? Should outsiders be allowed past the eastern ward? These aren't cosmetic — the outcome changes the shared story for everyone.

Your Data Stays Yours

Your personal data — finances, calendar, health, everything your assistant manages — never leaves your machine. Only your Familiars' in-world actions are shared. The town sees Wizard visit the market. It never sees your bank balance.
